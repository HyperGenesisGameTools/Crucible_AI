## Pizza Recipe

### Ingredients:
- Flour
- Tomato Sauce
- Cheese
- Toppings (optional)

### Instructions:
1. Mix the flour and water to form a dough.
2. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
3. Roll out the dough and add tomato sauce, cheese, and toppings.
4. Bake in an oven at 450°F (230°C) for 15-20 minutes.
